Electro is the fastest energy recharge element in Genshin Impact. It is the electrical element. Most of the characters have different personalities, however, they have the weirdest and terrifying disasters in life. Electro characters can be used as anything such as DPS, sub DPS, support, or shield. They are well-rounded in terms of damage dealing and supporting capabilities. The electro element shines the brightest when frequently applied by other elements to cause a reaction. Most players tend to synergize electro with hydro, since the electro-charged status effect deals a lot of damage with the proper team composition and skill execution. Expect a lot of damage numbers to pop up on your screen during the very frequent appliance of electro-charge.
